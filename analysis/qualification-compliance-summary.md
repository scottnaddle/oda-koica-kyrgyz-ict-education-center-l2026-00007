# Qualification / Compliance Summary

## Quick read
이 건의 가장 중요한 qualification 포인트는 다음이다.
- **Joint Venture: Not allowed**
- **Kyrgyz Republic 정부 산하 기관이 발급한 design license level 3 이상 필요**
- 입찰참가 entity는 자국 법령에 따라 적법하게 설립된 법인이어야 함
- bid security는 **입찰금액의 5% 이상**
- 기술평가 85점 이상이어야 협상 대상 진입 가능

즉, 전략 시그널은 강하지만 실제 참여는 매우 운영형/로컬 자격 중심이다.

- Submission deadline: 2026-04-16 15:00

- Submission address: 32, Razzakov str., Bishkek, the Kyrgyz Republic

- Bid price: lump-sum
- Currency: USD
- Standard language: English

## Eligibility requirements
### Entity / legal status
- Bidder must have submitted the application for participation by the deadline.
- Bidder must be a legitimate and lawful entity legally established under the laws of its own country.

### Local licensing requirement
- Bidder must hold a valid registration/license to provide design and engineering services for building construction.
- The BDS explicitly states **design license level 3 or higher issued by a state agency under the government of the Kyrgyz Republic**.

### Sanctions / integrity
- Bidder must not be under suspension by international organizations or governments.
- Korean firms must not be under Korean public procurement suspension.
- Bidder must not have been punished by any government on bribery charges.
- Anti-corruption / integrity pledges are required.

### Joint Venture / consortium
- **Joint Venture: Not allowed**
- This materially reduces flexibility for a foreign-led participation model.

## Evaluation / award structure
- Contract award: QCBS
- Technical 90% / Financial 10%
- Only bidders scoring **at least 85%** in technical evaluation become eligible for negotiation.
- Highest combined score enters pre-award negotiation first.

## Technical evaluation signals that matter
- PM qualifications are important enough that failure to meet PM qualification may downgrade evaluation.
- Company past performance must show similar completed design/engineering work in the last 5 years.
- Similar project examples include **new construction of office building, education or research institute building**.
- English capability of PM/personnel is explicitly relevant.

### What hurts

현재 기준으로는 **유비온 단독 또는 외국법인 주도형 직접 참여 가능성은 낮아 보인다.**
